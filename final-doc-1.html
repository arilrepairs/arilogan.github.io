Here's the big thing I would change:

Starting on page 5:
"The audience does not have ot be the intended audience; it includes anyone who would want to engage in rhetoric. 'Another part of the rhetorical context is audience, those who are the (intended or unintended) recipients of the rhetorical message.' 'In other words, the audience should be able to help address the problem' (Carroll, 2010, p.49)."

Which I would change to:
"The audience are those on the receiving end of the rhetoric. They don't have to be the intended audience, it also includes anyone who would want to engage in that rhetoric. Carroll (2010) gives a short description of audience: 'those who are the (intended or unintended) recipients of the rhetorical message.' She says that 'the audience should be able to help address the problem' (p.49)."
