I hope after reading my essay on discourse communities, you also have an understanding of the concept. These two "Other examples" are from a discourse community outside the English 101 discourse community. These are examples of my work from my Psychology 101 class which I felt showed my strengths.

This one shows how I take information from a source, and instead of just copy and pasting what I see, I add my own thoughts to it, so I can increase my understanding of the topic, along with the reader. Which is something I saw I did much better than my classmates from the Psychology 101 class.
