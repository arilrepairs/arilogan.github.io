Now this didn't become the final paper, but it was an important step to developing that final paper. Here's where I learned that working too hard at the start to be perfect, or to try and sound proper, is a nightmare when writing. It's really helped me be able to enjoy acedemic and professional writing, which I had grown to shy away from in the past because I would hyperfixate on trying to write a final draft from the start.
