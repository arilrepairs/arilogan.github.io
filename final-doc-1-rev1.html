This is where each essay began, with some sort of brainstorm.

Now, you might notice, but I did not follow up with this brainstorm in my final draft.

That's because of what I learned from doing this first brainstorm. Which it's always best to narrow the scope as much as you can when doing a deep-dive into a topic.

With trying to explain the rhetoric in the whole episode, there are simply too many things to talk about. Instead of trying to explain the rhetoric of say, the wedding plot, I also have to explain the b-plot of the episode and what that means and how it ties into the rest of the series. That's too much of a rabbit hole for one essay.

And pro tip for those looking for a topic to explain the rhetoric, it's super easy to get started if your topic is an advertisement.
