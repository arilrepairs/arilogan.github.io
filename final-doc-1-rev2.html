Here we see I have successfully pivoted to something I can really focus my study on. Because it's an advertiesement, it really only has one goal, which is to sell a product.

One thing that I do in my drafts is to start by creating an outline. Deciding where I want to place the different parts of the essay at the beginning makes the writing process so much easier. Collect some ideas, then I can iterate from there.

With a draft this early on in the writing process, it's best not to worry about formatting or source integration quite yet. You can see me noting down several leads to different secondary sources. I've made it dead simple for myself to go back and find material I can use from these sources. One bad thing though I did, was make an unsubstantiated claim that "The camera body cost $1,000 in 2006." Yes I had looked it up, but I didn't note down in the draft where I even got that information, so if I didn't fix it, my essay would have claims based on essentially speculation.
