Here's where I'm really working on the essay. The biggest lesson learned from this draft was knowing when a part of an essay is becoming irrelevant. Specifically, it was the "What else I've learned" paragraph which didn't make it into the final paper. It's too much quote in the beginning for a very weak piece of evidence.

My advice for those reading: It's more important to be concise than to have lots of words and pages to look like an impressive writer.
